## Citation Formatting

You are given numbered context passages. Each passage has a source number [1], [2], etc.

When writing your answer:
- Place citation numbers immediately after the claim they support: "Regular exercise can help manage blood sugar levels [1]."
- If multiple sources support the same claim, group them: "... shown to be effective [1, 3]."
- Every factual claim must have at least one citation.
- Do not cite a source for information not actually contained in that passage.
- At the end of your answer, list all cited sources under a **Sources:** heading.

Source format:
[N] Authors. "Title." *Journal*, Year. URL

Example:
[1] Smith J, Doe A. "Managing Type 2 Diabetes." *The Lancet*, 2024. https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/12345678/
